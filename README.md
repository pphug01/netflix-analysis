# Netflix Catalog Analysis

**Objective:** Analyze Netflix’s content to uncover trends in type, genres, geography, and catalog growth.

**Dataset:** 8807 titles with movies and TV shows, including genre, country, rating, and release date.

**Key Insights:**
- Movies dominate (6131) vs TV Shows (2676)
- US dominates production, with strong contributions from India & UK
- Most popular genres: International Movies, Dramas, Comedies
- Movie duration: 80–120 min
- Catalog growth exploded 2016–2020

**Business Implications:**
- International content is strategic
- Dramas and comedies appeal to wide audiences
- TV Shows key for retention
